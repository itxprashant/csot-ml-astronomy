## How to Use This List

You will not read all of this. Suggested workflow for Week 4:

1. **Part 1:** watch the PBS Space Time lensing video, then browse the CASTLES gallery and a couple of ESA/Hubble lens images to build your visual template.
2. **Part 2:** skim the OpenAI CLIP blog post and the Hugging Face zero-shot task page before opening the notebook.
3. **Build** the zero-shot scorer from the starter before reading more.
4. **Metrics:** watch one StatQuest ROC/AUC video and skim the scikit-learn precision-recall example.
5. **After the week:** read the Strong Lens Finding Challenge paper to see how researchers benchmark real lens finders — and how hard the problem genuinely is.
